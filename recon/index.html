Recon Engines

Calc Engine

Pricing configuration for a transaction. i.e: Minimum and maximum fees, fixed fee, country, payment methods, FX, tiers and more.
Consider taxes like VAT, GST.

Reserve Engine

The reserve process is acting on the settlement records that were calculated.
A cut from the settlement is taken and transferred to the reserve wallet.

Settlement Engine

After the calculations, funds are being deducted from the wallets and transfered to the merchants.

Billing Engine

The billing process is the phase in which Rapyd gets its cut in the form of fees.
The fees are calculated from the transactions that a merchant made.
